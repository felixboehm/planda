# Research Documentation

This folder contains platform and competitor research conducted during the product discovery phase.

## Platform Analysis

Analysis of existing platforms and their approaches to community building, governance, and engagement:

- **[dao-governance-analysis.md](./dao-governance-analysis.md)** - Research on DAO governance models, token-based systems, and decentralized decision-making
- **[linkedin-analysis.md](./linkedin-analysis.md)** - Professional networking features and community engagement patterns
- **[meetup-analysis.md](./meetup-analysis.md)** - Local event organization and community building features
- **[reddit-analysis.md](./reddit-analysis.md)** - Community moderation, discussion threading, and engagement mechanics
- **[skool-analysis.md](./skool-analysis.md)** - Online learning communities and course management approaches

## Purpose

These research documents inform TopLocs' product development by:
- Identifying successful patterns in community building
- Understanding governance and trust mechanisms
- Discovering feature gaps and opportunities
- Validating multi-identity and location-interest concepts
